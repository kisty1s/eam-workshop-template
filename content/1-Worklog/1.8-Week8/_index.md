The eighth week of the internship focused on implementing enterprise-level features, reporting utilities, and intelligent user assistance systems. The core development activities included building electronic signature workflows, customer satisfaction (CSAT) evaluation forms, progress trackers, and document export modules. ### Week 8 Objectives:

* Build structural workflow utilities including visual progress trackers, CSAT feedback panels, and self-service FAQs.
* Develop data export tools supporting both PDF and Excel formats for administrative auditing.
* Integrate a hybrid support dialogue interface utilizing Gemini AI automated responses and live administrative chat channels.
