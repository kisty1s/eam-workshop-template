The first week of the internship primarily focused on getting acquainted with the internship environment at Amazon Web Services Vietnam, exploring the learning roadmap for Cloud Computing, and building a foundational knowledge of cloud service models. Additionally, initial research and configurations were conducted on the AWS Management Console to set up a secure and cost-managed cloud account layout.

### Week 1 Objectives:
* Acquire foundational knowledge of Cloud Computing and understand different cloud service models (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS).
* Explore and become familiar with the AWS Management Console interface.
* Register for an AWS account and thoroughly research security regulations and cost-management policies.
* Establish a foundation for learning and reporting to align with the core objectives of the internship program.
