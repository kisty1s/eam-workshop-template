The third week of the internship shifted focus from networking layers to cloud storage solutions and identity management on AWS. The core content included conducting theoretical research and hands-on practices on Amazon S3 object storage, structuring data buckets, and managing resource permissions. Additionally, foundational configurations were carried out using AWS Identity and Access Management (IAM) to manage users, groups, and access controls securely.

### Week 3 Objectives:
* Deeply research the core concepts and operational parameters of Amazon S3 (Simple Storage Service).
* Practice initializing S3 Buckets, uploading object data, and configuring bucket access permissions.
* Familiarize with AWS Identity and Access Management (IAM) infrastructure layout.
* Understand how to manage IAM Users, Groups, and Roles to maintain secure access across AWS resources.
